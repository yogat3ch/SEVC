The Southeast Vipassana Center, Dhamma Patāpa, is located in Jesup, GA, which is in the South Central part of Georgia in Wayne County. It is located on a property with beautiful old pine trees, oaks, and other varieties of trees and vegetation. The beauty and seclusion of this property makes for an ideal meditation environment which will serve students in the Southeast for years to come.

Instructions for Rideshare:

  The Southeast Vipassana Center generates a separate rideshare board for each course, available for use by participants accepted into that course. Please check your acceptance email for the rideshare board link to your course, and for details on updating the information on the rideshare board. For any further assistance, contact the rideshare coordinators at rides@patapa.dhamma.org

Traveling by Car: Please follow the directions provided via Google Maps above, the printer friendly directions below, or by GPS using the special instructions given below. Please call the center at (912) 588-9807 if you get lost. By GPS: Because GPS unit maps often cannot accurately locate the Southeast Vipassana Center address, set your GPS unit to the intersection of Broadhurst Rd E and Rogers Break. Rogers Break only goes in one direction from the intersection. Turn on Rogers Break, drive for about 0.5 miles. Look for the Southeast Vipassana Center sign on the right. From there, follow the signs to the Registration Building. Alternatively set the GPS to the Southeast Vipassana Center driveway on Rogers Break (31.468469 long,-81.9058362 lat) and look for the signs to the center when nearing the destination.

Public Transportation

Bus Transport:

Greyhound Service is available to: Hinesville / Fleming, GA (38 miles from the Center) Brunswick, GA (44 miles from the Center) From both of these towns, travelers will need to get a taxi to take them to the Center. Below is the taxi service information for both of these towns. Please call several days in advance to check availability and make a reservation. Hinesville, GA: M&L Cab 916-877-5022; approximately $60 Brunswick, GA: Metro Cab - (912) 554-2000; approximately $80

Train Transport:

Option 1: Travelers arriving at the Jesup Amtrak station should call the center at (912)588-9807 a few days before arrival to notify the management team of the date and arrival time. We may be able to transport students to the Center Option 2: Call Wayne County Transit a few days ahead of arrival 912-427-5914. Depending on their schedule, they may be able to provide transportation. Cost is $6.00 one way Option 3: There are no taxi services in Jesup but there is one in Hinesville that will come to Jesup: M&L Cab 916-877-5022. Must schedule ahead of arrival

Air Transport:

Savannah, GA (Savannah/ Hilton Head International Airport (SAV))- There are 2 taxi companies that will drive from the airport directly to the Center:
  1. A1 Taxi - 912-925-9900; approximately $115, each additional person $5-10, up to 8 people, needs 1 day notice
  2. Magikal Taxi - 912-897-8294; approximately $120, up to 3 people, needs 1 or 2 days notice
Jacksonville, FL (Jacksonville International Airport (JAX)) - ABC Limo Service will take people from the airport directly to the Center for approximately $160 for 1-2 people, 3rd person is additional $50.
